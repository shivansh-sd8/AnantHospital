Gynecology Department

About Gynecology

Gynecology is the medical practice dealing with the health of the female reproductive system. It includes diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to pregnancy, menstruation, fertility, and menopause.

Advanced Treatments

Our department specializes in treatments such as high-risk pregnancy management, laparoscopic surgery, infertility treatments, and gynecological cancer care.
