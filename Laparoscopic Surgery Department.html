About Laparoscopic Surgery

Laparoscopic surgery is a minimally invasive surgical technique used to perform operations with small incisions and specialized tools, ensuring faster recovery and reduced post-operative complications.

Advanced Treatments

Our department specializes in advanced laparoscopic procedures, including gallbladder removal, hernia repair, appendectomy, and bariatric surgery.
