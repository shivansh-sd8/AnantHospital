Plastic Surgery Department

About Plastic Surgery

Plastic surgery is a specialized medical field focused on reconstructing and enhancing physical appearance through surgical and non-surgical procedures. It includes cosmetic enhancements, reconstructive surgery, and treatments for congenital abnormalities, burns, and injuries.

Advanced Treatments

Our department specializes in state-of-the-art procedures, including rhinoplasty, facelifts, liposuction, reconstructive surgeries, and minimally invasive techniques for aesthetic and medical improvements.
