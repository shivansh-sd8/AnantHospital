About Pulmonary Medicine

Pulmonology is a medical specialty that deals with diseases involving the respiratory tract. Our pulmonologists are experts in diagnosing and treating conditions of the chest, particularly pneumonia, asthma, tuberculosis, emphysema, and complicated chest infections.

We provide care for patients with diseases of the lungs and respiratory system, from common conditions like bronchitis to complex illnesses such as pulmonary fibrosis. Our approach combines the latest diagnostic tools with evidence-based treatments to optimize respiratory health.

Our center is equipped with advanced technology including pulmonary function testing equipment, bronchoscopy suites, and sleep study facilities to provide comprehensive care for all respiratory conditions.
