Nephrology (Nephro) Department

About Nephrology (Nephro)

Nephrology focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of kidney-related diseases. Our experts provide advanced care for conditions such as chronic kidney disease, kidney stones, and hypertension-related kidney disorders.

Advanced Diagnostic Techniques

We use state-of-the-art imaging, dialysis procedures, and kidney function tests to ensure precise diagnosis and treatment.
