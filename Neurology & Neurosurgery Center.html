About Our Department

Our Neurology and Neurosurgery Department provides comprehensive care for patients with disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. We combine advanced diagnostic tools with cutting-edge treatments to deliver the best possible outcomes.

Our team includes board-certified neurologists and neurosurgeons who specialize in various areas of neurological care, from medical management to complex surgical interventions.
